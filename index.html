About Us

Welcome to Huke Upholstery! We are a family-owned business with over 30 years of experience in providing top-notch upholstery services. Our team of skilled craftsmen is dedicated to transforming your furniture with quality fabrics and meticulous attention to detail.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of upholstery services to meet all your needs, including:

  • Re-Upholstery
  • Traditional Upholstery
  • Loose covers (Washable)
  • Replacement cushion inners
  • Re-springing
  • Repairs
